Description

What

Create a one-page battle card that gives any SDR or AE everything needed to run the Productboard displacement play without improvising.

Why now

Productboard's June layoffs left many of its accounts without the champion who bought it, and its Salesforce integration is gated to the Enterprise tier — two openings our win/loss notes confirm. The window closes as those accounts re-commit at renewal.

User story

As an SDR or AE, I want one card with the targeting rule, the pain hooks, and the price counter, so that I can run the play confidently without escalating to product marketing.

The card must cover
  • Who to target — accounts with a recent champion role-change
  • The two pain hooks, each with sourced proof points
  • The price-counter script — never opens with a discount
  • Landmines — where Productboard honestly wins, and we disqualify
Out of scope

Pricing changes · outbound sequence copy (that's T-5).

Grounding: PRD § 5 R2 · displacement win-rate baseline [NEED: not yet tracked]

Acceptance criteria — 6

Given a target account with a recent champion role-change, When the SDR opens the card, Then it provides the role-change talk track with at least two sourced proof points.
Given a mid-market account on Productboard, When the Salesforce lock-in hook is used, Then the card states the Enterprise-gating fact with its source and date — no unsourced claims.
Given a price objection, When the AE follows the counter script, Then the response reframes on value and consolidation and never opens with a discount.
Given a scenario where Productboard genuinely fits better, When the SDR checks the landmines section, Then the card names it and instructs disqualification.
[edge] Given a claim whose evidence is missing, When the card is generated, Then the claim renders as [NEED: source] — never asserted without one.
[failure] Given the card is older than 30 days or a cited fact changes, When an SDR opens it, Then a staleness banner appears and re-verification is requested.
