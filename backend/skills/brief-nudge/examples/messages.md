## What stays fixed vs. what flexes
- **Fixed:** one dominant CTA; impact-led subject/title/headline; the deep link to workspace → brief; Slack compact / email titled; the honesty pass.
- **Flexes:** how many items appear (3 → 2 → 1 → 1 across the days); the tone (announce → impact-led → cost-of-waiting → final); whether the email shows the secondary "jump straight in" links (Day 0 only here).
- **Never:** a number without a backing field in the brief; manufactured scarcity; a reminder after the brief is opened or after the Day-3 pause.
