Brief Nudge — getting the weekly brief opened

The delivery layer for weekly-brief: a Day-0 announcement plus Day-1/2/3 reminders (sent only if unopened), on Slack and email. Switch days to compare. Recipient and figures are illustrative.

One dominant CTA, not three. Every message drives a single action — Open this week's brief, deep-linked to the workspace → brief page. The top items are a scannable teaser, not competing buttons. Email adds an optional, quiet "jump straight in" row for power users (see Day 0).
