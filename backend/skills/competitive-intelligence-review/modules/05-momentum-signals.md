# Module 5 — Momentum signals (who's *actually* winning + where the trend goes)

**Question:** Beyond features, who has momentum — in traffic, app adoption, AI-search visibility, and shipping speed — and where is the category heading?

**Why.** Share is a lagging snapshot; momentum is the leading signal. A smaller player gaining traffic + ratings + ship velocity is the real threat. This module is also the **scenario-modeling** step (where it's going), not just where it is.

**Signals to pull (see data-sources.md for free workarounds):**
1. **Web traffic & trend** — relative visits and direction (growing/declining). *Free:* Similarweb free tier (top-line visits), Google Trends (search interest over time, head-to-head), Ahrefs/Ubersuggest free keyword volume.
2. **App adoption** (if mobile) — downloads/rank trend + rating count velocity. *Free:* public App Store / Google Play rankings & rating counts over time; Sensor Tower / data.ai free snapshots.
3. **AI-search visibility (new + essential)** — when a user asks ChatGPT/Perplexity "best tool for <category>", do you appear, and who's cited? *Free:* run the actual prompts across ChatGPT/Claude/Perplexity/Gemini and tally brand mention share + citation share + sentiment. This is a real and growing acquisition channel; absence is a closable gap.
4. **Ship cadence** — how often each ships, and what. *Free:* changelog/release-notes pages, GitHub releases, blog "what's new", X/LinkedIn announcements. Count releases/quarter and theme them.
5. **Hiring & investment signal** — open roles (where they're staffing = where they're betting), funding/announcements. *Free:* careers pages, LinkedIn, Crunchbase free.

**Output:** a momentum scorecard per player (traffic trend, app trend, AI-search share, ship cadence, hiring focus) + a **"where the category is going"** read synthesized from the above.
**Hand to module 8:** who's gaining/fading; the trend we must get ahead of; the AI-search gap.
**Confidence:** free-tier traffic/app numbers are 🅢 directional estimates — never cite them as exact. AI-search and ship-cadence tallies you run yourself are 🅗.
